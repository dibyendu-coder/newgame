# 🧩 Mind Refresher Game

A fun and interactive web-based game designed to refresh your mind and test your mental abilities. Choose from three engaging game modes to keep your brain sharp!

## 🎮 Game Modes

### 1. **Memory Match** 🧠
- Classic memory card matching game
- Flip cards to find matching pairs
- Difficulty increases with each level
- Perfect for improving focus and memory

**How to Play:**
- Click on cards to flip them
- Match two identical symbols
- Complete all pairs to advance to the next level
- Try to do it in minimum moves!

### 2. **Sequence** 📊
- Simon Says-style color sequence game
- Watch the pattern and repeat it back
- Pattern gets longer with each round
- Speed increases as you progress

**How to Play:**
- Watch the color sequence carefully
- Click the buttons in the exact same order
- Each correct round adds a new color to the sequence
- How many levels can you reach?

### 3. **Quick Quiz** ❓
- Fast-paced multiple-choice questions
- Various topics: Math, Geography, Science
- Beat the clock and rack up points
- Test your knowledge and quick thinking

**How to Play:**
- Read the question carefully
- Choose from 4 options
- Correct answers earn points
- Combo multiplier for consecutive correct answers

## 🎯 Game Tips

**Memory Match:**
- Try to memorize the position of cards
- Start with fewer cards and work your way up
- Focus on patterns

**Sequence:**
- Watch carefully as the sequence plays
- Repeat immediately after watching
- Say the colors out loud to help remember

**Quick Quiz:**
- Read the full question before answering
- Don't rush, think carefully
- Learn from your mistakes

## 📊 Scoring System

- **Memory Match**: 10 points per match × level multiplier
- **Sequence**: 10 points per correct round × level multiplier
- **Quiz**: 5 points per correct answer × level multiplier
- **Combo Bonus**: Building combo streaks increases your score multiplier
